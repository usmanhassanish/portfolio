MEET GWANI
Man of the People


Gwani Mohammed Alqadi is a dedicated community leader, entrepreneur, and advocate for progress, proudly rooted in the heart of Jos. Known for his unwavering commitment to justice, development, and inclusive governance, Mohammed has spent years working at the grassroots level to empower youth, support small businesses, and promote unity across diverse communities.

With a vision for a more prosperous and equitable Jos, he brings a fresh, people-centered approach to leadership—one that listens, learns, and leads with integrity. Mohammed believes in practical solutions, transparent governance, and the power of community to drive lasting change.

As he steps forward to serve, Mohammed Alqadi stands not as a politician, but as a servant of the people—ready to champion the hopes and needs of every citizen.
